About Nepal News Hub

🇳🇵 Our Mission

Nepal News Hub is dedicated to providing fast, reliable, and unbiased news from across Nepal. We aggregate content from 21+ trusted news sources to give you comprehensive coverage in one place.

📰 What We Offer

  • Real-time news updates from 21+ sources
  • Bilingual support (English & Nepali)
  • Category-wise filtering (Politics, Business, Sports, Tech, Health)
  • Trending news detection
  • Bookmark & share features
  • Data-saver mode for slow connections

👥 Our Team

We are a small team of tech enthusiasts and journalism advocates based in Kathmandu, working to make news more accessible to all Nepalis.
